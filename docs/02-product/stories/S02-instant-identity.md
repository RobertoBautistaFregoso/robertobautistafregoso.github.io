# Story S02: Instant identity (hero)
As a visitor, I want to grasp who Roberto is and what he does almost immediately on landing, so that I can quickly decide whether he's relevant to me.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Given I land on the home page, when it loads, then Roberto's name and a one-sentence positioning statement are visible above the fold without scrolling.
- Given the hero, when I read it, then it states his role (AI Product Manager) plus at least one concrete specific (a domain, a notable result, or a focus area) — i.e., the positioning line is not solely a generic job title.
- Given the hero loads, when content is still arriving, then no layout shift moves the headline after I've started reading (stable above-the-fold render).

## Non-goals
- Full bio / about narrative (lives in the about section + resume page)
- Decorative animations or hero imagery (can come via iteration)
