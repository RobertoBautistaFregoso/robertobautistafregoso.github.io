# Story S06: Reach out (CTA)
As a convinced visitor, I want a clear, easy way to contact Roberto, so that I can act on my interest without hunting for how.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Given any point on the home page, when I decide to reach out, then a clear contact CTA is reachable without searching (e.g., persistent nav link and/or a contact section).
- Given the CTA, when I activate it, then it initiates contact through the chosen channel (e.g., `mailto:` opens my email client, or it links to the designated profile) — exact mechanism decided in the Phase 3 ADR.
- Given the CTA, when it is displayed, then it is visually distinct and reads as actionable (clearly a button/link, not body text).

## Non-goals
- Server-side contact form with submission handling (out of scope for static v0)
- Scheduling/calendar integration
