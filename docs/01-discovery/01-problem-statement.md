# Problem Statement: Portfolio Site (v0)

**Date:** 2026-05-25
**Project:** First portfolio site — built through the agentic SDLC, reference vibe: itspatmorgan.com
**Method:** Problem Framing Narrative (lightweight Phase 1 — problem is well-understood, heavy MITRE canvas skipped)

---

## Problem Framing Narrative

**I am:**
A hiring manager at an AI company, a prospective consulting client, or a peer in the AI/product community — landing on Roberto's site with ~20 seconds of attention.

**Trying to:**
Quickly decide whether Roberto is an exceptional AI product person — real capability, a distinct point of view, and a clear trajectory — worth interviewing, hiring, or engaging.

**But:**
There's no fast, trustworthy signal. LinkedIn lists titles; it doesn't *show* his agentic-AI capability, his perspective, or where he's headed. I can't tell him apart from a hundred other "AI PMs."

**Because:**
He has no portfolio that demonstrates capability with evidence, expresses his POV, and shows his direction — especially the agentic build-in-public skill most relevant to AI-native roles right now.

**Which makes me feel:**
Unconvinced, and likely to move on without reaching out.

---

## Final Problem Statement

> Decision-makers in AI and product need a fast, credible way to judge whether Roberto is an exceptional AI product builder — with a distinct POV and clear trajectory — but a generic LinkedIn shows none of that, so he stays undifferentiated and the people who'd engage him move on without reaching out.

---

## Context & Constraints

- **≤ 3 screens, no auth.** Convey identity + proof + a *seed* of POV + trajectory — without ballooning into a content platform.
- **Second user = me (Roberto).** The site is *living*, iterated continuously via the agentic GitHub-native workflow. Architecture must make adding a project or POV piece cheap.
- **Voice mirrors who I am** — personality, not a sterile template.
- **The public repo + workflow trail is part of the product** — the build process (feature branches, PRs, issues, project board) is itself part of what the site demonstrates.
- **Hosting likely GitHub Pages** (on-theme with the GitHub-native story) — decided in Phase 3.

---

## Positioning (v0)

Kept **open**: optimize for "Roberto is an exceptional AI product builder." Let the specific path — full-time role (incl. frontier AI labs) vs. consulting engagement — follow from who shows up. No need to commit to one at the portfolio level in v0.

---

## The unifying insight

The site **is the proof**. Building it through the agentic SDLC, in public, with a clean PR/issue/board trail, is the single strongest artifact for all audiences — it doesn't just *claim* agentic-AI capability, it *demonstrates* it. This project and the SDLC learning goal are the same thing.
