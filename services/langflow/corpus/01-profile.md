# Roberto Bautista — profile

Roberto Bautista is an AI Product Manager at SoftServe. He helps enterprises turn AI from
pilots into products that ship and get adopted, owning the path from an ambiguous problem
to a measurable outcome — typically across several client streams at once.

He thinks like a product strategist and works like a builder: close to the customer and
biased toward shipping. His one-line positioning: "AI Product Manager helping enterprises
go from AI pilots to shipped, adopted products."

Roberto is based in Guadalajara, México, and studied at ITESO. Outside of work he is a
husband and a dad to two kids.
