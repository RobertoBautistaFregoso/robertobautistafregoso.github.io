# Roberto — skills and how he works

## What he does
Roberto is an AI Product Manager. He owns the path from an ambiguous business problem to a
shipped, adopted, measurable AI product: problem framing, discovery, delivery, and
adoption — usually across several concurrent enterprise client streams. He translates vague
business problems into products that ship and can be measured.

## AI / GenAI depth
Roberto works hands-on with production GenAI system design, not just from the sidelines. He
studies and applies retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and when it beats fine-tuning,
evaluation design for non-deterministic LLM systems, agentic patterns and tool use, and the
cost/latency tradeoffs that decide what actually ships. He practices a spec-driven, agentic
software development workflow (the agentic SDLC) end to end and has built his own portfolio
site that way.

## Product craft
- Product discovery and problem framing; turning ambiguity into scoped, measurable work.
- Shipping MVPs that move real metrics (e.g., an instant-claims MVP that cut claim time up
  to 5×; a KPI dashboard that cut man-hours 20%).
- Partnering closely with engineering and design to deliver.
- Growth: built a first sales team (conversion 2% → 10%), grew revenue 5×, launched
  referral and omnichannel programs.
- Operations leadership: ran a 150+ person unit, applied Lean, improved efficiency/quality.

## How he works
He thinks like a product strategist and works like a builder — close to the customer,
biased toward shipping, and willing to do the hard, unglamorous work to make something
real. He would rather understand how a thing works than admire it from a distance, which is
why he stays hands-on with the technical craft rather than only managing it.

## Tools and methods seen in his work
AI product management, GenAI system design, RAG, LLM evaluation, agentic SDLC, Claude Code,
Astro, GitHub Actions, product discovery, PRDs and user stories, architecture decision
records, Lean, KPI dashboards and BI.
