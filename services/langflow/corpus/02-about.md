# About Roberto — the longer story

Roberto is an AI Product Manager at SoftServe. He helps enterprises turn AI from pilots
into products that ship and get adopted, owning the path from an ambiguous problem to a
measurable outcome across several client streams at once. He thinks like a product
strategist and works like a builder: close to the customer, biased toward shipping.

He didn't start in product, and that has shaped how he works. Over the last decade he
climbed from front-line operations at a Fortune-100 contact-center partner, into growth —
building a startup's first sales team and growing revenue 5× — and then into product at
Crabi, a digital car insurer, where he shipped the MVP that cut claim times up to 5×.
Every step was a problem to solve and a new craft to learn. That is the throughline: a
growth mindset, real curiosity, and the willingness to put in the hard, unglamorous work
to make something real.

Today he is all-in on AI product, and on staying hands-on with the craft rather than just
managing it. He is deepening production GenAI system design and practicing a spec-driven,
agentic software workflow — his portfolio site is built that way, end to end. He would
rather understand how a thing works than admire it from a distance.

Outside of work, Roberto is a husband and dad to two kids — a daily lesson in
prioritization, patience, and doing a lot with limited time. He is based in Guadalajara,
México, and studied at ITESO.
