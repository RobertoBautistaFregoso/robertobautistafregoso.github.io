# Roberto's experience — Crabi (2019–2022)

Crabi is a user-centric digital car insurer in Latin America. It uses mobile and web apps
plus driving-behavior data to personalize premiums. Roberto held two roles there.

## Product Manager (Aug 2020 – Jul 2022)
- Built the first MVP of instant claim management, reducing claim time up to 5× while
  driving 4.8/5 customer satisfaction.
- Built a claim-management and product dashboard for real-time KPI tracking, reducing
  man-hours by 20%.
- Led the re-architecture that let users manage multiple policies from a single account;
  post-launch user acquisition grew 20%.

## Growth Manager (Oct 2019 – Jul 2020)
- Grew monthly revenue 5×.
- Built the first sales team from scratch, lifting conversion from 2% to 10%.
- Implemented the first omnichannel platform, automating WhatsApp communication and
  increasing engagement.
- Launched the company's first word-of-mouth referral program, increasing referrals by 50%.
